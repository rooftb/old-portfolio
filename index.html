About me

Greetings.

My name is Tanner B. Roof and I am a Front End Developer. I started out writing HTML in Notepad at the age of twelve. By the time I was fourteen, I was hosting a successful video game modding website with my own dedicated server space.

I graduated from the University of South Carolina in 2014 with a B.A. in Journalism & Mass Communication. Since then, I joined a cohort of fellow developers at The Iron Yard to hone my web development skills. Three rigourous months of training later and I went from making rudimentary static sites to responsive mobile-first ones built in Javascript frameworks. Even after graduating, I still hone my skills constantly.
